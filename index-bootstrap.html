Translit Keyboard

Do you often type in another language but find yourself looking down at your phone whenever you switch away from the QWERTY English keyboard? Transliteration is the solution to your problem! Instead of having to memorize multiple keyboard layouts, simply type things out phonetically.

Live Transliteration

Don't wait to transliterate! Just start typing and watch the transliteration happen in real time. There's no button pushing and no extra work. And if you need to switch back to English, simply hit the toggle at the top of your keyboard and type away. You can always consult the transliteration mappings for your language in the container app.

3rd Party Keyboard

Translit Keyboard is a fully integrated iOS system keyboard. This means that you can use it in practically any app (with a few exceptions). Text your friends; browse the web; write down your babushka's recipies, all in the original language.

Multiple Languages

Multiple languages are supported for transliteration. With the premium app, you get Russian, blah, blah, blah, blah. Or you can try the Lite app with Old Slavonic.

Full Privacy

With Translit Keyboard, you can be sure that your privacy is never violated. The app is intentionally made to never request full acess to your data, meaning that nothing you do in the keyboard is ever available to me, the developer. You can find the full privacy policy here.
